FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HealinBelieve?

HealinBelieve is a medical tourism platform that connects international patients with accredited hospitals and specialist doctors across India, Germany, Thailand, South Korea, and Turkey. We provide treatment search, cost comparison, AI doctor matching, appointment booking, and all-inclusive travel packages.

How does the AI doctor matching work?

Our AI algorithm evaluates your needs across four factors: specialty match (35%), budget compatibility (25%), doctor experience (25%), and location convenience (15%). It scores every doctor in our database and ranks them by overall compatibility, showing detailed percentage breakdowns.

Are the hospitals verified?

Yes. Every hospital on HealinBelieve is reviewed and approved by our admin team. We only list hospitals with international accreditations such as JCI, NABH, ISO, or government certifications.

How do I book an appointment?

Create a free patient account, browse hospitals or use our AI matcher, then click "Book Appointment" on any hospital page. Choose your doctor, treatment, and preferred date. The hospital will confirm or reschedule within 24–48 hours.

What is included in a medical tourism package?

Packages vary but typically include: the medical treatment/procedure, hotel accommodation near the hospital, airport pickup and drop-off, translator services, and post-treatment follow-up care. Check individual package details for specifics.

Can I compare treatment costs?

Absolutely! Our Cost Comparison tool lets you compare treatment prices across different hospitals and countries. Filter by category to see side-by-side pricing, duration, and success rates.

Do I need a medical visa?

This depends on your nationality and destination country. We provide country-specific visa guides on our Visa Assistance page. Many of our partner hospitals assist with visa invitation letters.

Is my medical data secure?

Yes. We use secure authentication with hashed passwords, encrypted data transmission, and role-based access control. Only you and your assigned hospital can view your medical records.

Can I upload my medical records?

Yes. Once logged in, go to your Patient Dashboard and use the upload feature to securely share PDFs, images, or documents with your hospital.

How do hospitals join the platform?

Hospitals can register as a "Hospital / Provider" account. After submitting their profile, our admin team reviews the information and approves verified hospitals to appear on the platform.
