Medical Tourism Travel Guide

Everything you need to know before your medical journey

✈️ Before You Travel

  • Research: Use HealinBelieve to compare hospitals, doctors, and packages
  • Consultation: Book a virtual consultation with your doctor before travelling
  • Documents: Gather medical records, imaging, lab results — translate if needed
  • Insurance: Purchase international medical and travel insurance
  • Visa: Apply for a medical visa with your hospital's invitation letter
  • Budget: Plan for treatment, accommodation, food, and local transport

🏨 Accommodation Tips

  • Many of our packages include hotel accommodation near the hospital
  • Look for serviced apartments for longer stays — more affordable and comfortable
  • Request a hospital-adjacent hotel for post-surgery convenience
  • Confirm if your hotel offers wheelchair accessibility if needed

🌍 Country Highlights

🇮🇳 India

Known for affordable cardiac, orthopedic, and transplant surgery. Top hospitals in Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi.

🇩🇪 Germany

Leading in oncology, neurology, and precision medicine. World-renowned research hospitals in Berlin.

🇹🇭 Thailand

Top destination for cosmetic surgery, dental care, and wellness. Beautiful recovery environment in Bangkok.

🇰🇷 South Korea

Advanced robotic surgery, ophthalmology, and aesthetics. Korean government actively supports medical tourism.

🇹🇷 Turkey

Affordable hair transplants, dental implants, and oncology. Istanbul bridges Europe and Asia.

💡 Recovery Tips

  • Follow your doctor's post-procedure instructions strictly
  • Plan to stay 2–5 extra days after your treatment before flying home
  • Avoid long flights within 48 hours of surgery — check with your doctor
  • Use telemedicine follow-ups after returning home
  • Keep all medical documentation for follow-up care in your home country
