Medical Visa Guide

Information about medical visas for our partner countries

🇮🇳 India Medical Visa

  • Type: Medical Visa (MV) — valid for up to 1 year
  • Required: Invitation letter from hospital, medical records, passport, photos
  • Processing: 5–10 business days
  • e-Visa: Available for 160+ nationalities — apply online at indianvisaonline.gov.in
  • Companion: Up to 2 medical attendants allowed on Medical-X visa

🇩🇪 Germany Medical Visa

  • Type: Schengen Visa (medical purpose) — valid for up to 90 days
  • Required: Hospital appointment confirmation, proof of funds, insurance, passport
  • Processing: 10–15 business days
  • Note: EU/EEA citizens do not need a visa

🇹🇭 Thailand Medical Visa

  • Type: Medical Treatment Visa (Non-O-ME) — valid for 90 days, extendable
  • Required: Hospital acceptance letter, financial proof, passport
  • Visa-Free: Many nationalities get 30-day visa-free entry (sufficient for most treatments)
  • Processing: 5–7 business days

🇰🇷 South Korea Medical Visa

  • Type: Medical Tourism Visa (C-3-3 or G-1-10)
  • Required: Hospital reservation, treatment plan, proof of funds
  • Processing: 7–10 business days
  • Benefit: Government-supported medical tourism programme with welcome centres at airports

🇹🇷 Turkey Medical Visa

  • Type: Medical Purpose Visa or e-Visa
  • Required: Hospital invitation, passport, photos, financial proof
  • e-Visa: Available for many nationalities — quick online application
  • Processing: 3–5 business days

📝 General Tips

  • Start the visa process at least 4–6 weeks before your planned travel date
  • Ask your chosen hospital for an official invitation/acceptance letter
  • Carry all medical documentation in English (translated and notarized if needed)
  • Purchase comprehensive travel & medical insurance before departure
  • HealinBelieve partner hospitals can assist with visa invitation letters
